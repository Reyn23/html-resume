About Me

I’m a passionate dancer/ Coach choreographer and performer who teaches dance, and compete at national and international levels. I’m motivated by growth, discipline, and the excitement of putting my best skills on stage in high-level competitions.

Outside of dance, I enjoy watching movies and anime, using them as a way to unwind and stay inspired creatively. I also maintain a focus on health and fitness—I like running and going to the gym to stay active and fit.

Recently, though, I’ve had to pause lifting due to a motorcycle accident. Even with that setback, I’m still committed to staying fit in the ways I can and to continuing my routines as I recover. I also love fashion, with a strong preference for classic attire and street styles.
